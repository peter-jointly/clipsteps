🛒 Ingredients (just 4)

  • A pork joint with good skin — check it in the supermarket; poor skin means poor crackling
  • Rapeseed oil (any neutral oil works)
  • Sea salt flakes
  • Black pepper — just a little

🧰 You'll also need · ⏱ Timing

  • Roasting tin, meat thermometer, pastry brush, kitchen paper
  • Total time about 4½ hours: 20 min hot + ~3 h slow + 20 min hot + 30 min rest — almost all hands-off
  • No other flavourings needed — pork has plenty of its own
