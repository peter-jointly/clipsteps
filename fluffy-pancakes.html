🛒 Ingredients (all pantry staples)

  • Dry: plain flour, sugar, salt, baking powder and baking soda (bi-carb) — both, that's the fluff
  • Wet: milk + a splash of apple cider vinegar (instant "buttermilk"), neutral oil, vanilla, eggs
  • To cook & serve: salted butter (Frank uses whipped salted) and real maple syrup
  • Frank's exact amounts flash up on screen as he adds each one — pause at that moment to catch them. Typical amounts for this style if you miss them: 2 cups flour · 2 tbsp sugar · 2 tsp baking powder · ½ tsp bi-carb · ½ tsp salt · 2 cups milk + 2 tbsp vinegar · 2 eggs · ¼ cup oil · 1 tsp vanilla

🧰 Equipment

  • Two mixing bowls and a whisk
  • Electric griddle if you have one; otherwise a non-stick frying pan or cast-iron griddle works fine
  • A plastic spatula (metal scratches non-stick) and a big spoon or ice-cream scoop for the batter
