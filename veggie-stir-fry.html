🛒 Ingredients — ✓ from Nagi's written recipe (serves 4)

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped · 1 tbsp ginger, finely sliced (optional)
  • ½ onion, peeled and sliced
  • 1 large carrot · 1 capsicum, sliced ½ cm · 1 cup mushrooms, sliced 3 mm
  • 3 medium buk choy or other leafy greens of choice — our version swaps this slot for the box's best sellers: broccoli, green beans and snowpeas (see the box panel and Steps 4–5)
  • Sauce (all pantry):
    • 1 tbsp cornflour / corn starch
    • 1½ tbsp light or all-purpose soy sauce (not dark soy · gluten-free: use tamari)
    • 2 tsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine or Mirin (subs: dry sherry, cooking sake; non-alcoholic — swap the water for low-sodium stock)
    • ½ tsp sesame oil (optional) · dash of white pepper (or black)
    • ¾ cup (185 ml) water
  • To serve: finely sliced green onions, sesame seeds, rice

🧰 Equipment · ⏱ Timing

  • A sharp knife and board, plus a jug or bowl to mix the sauce in

  • Serves 4 — as a main over rice, or as a veggie side with anything Asian
