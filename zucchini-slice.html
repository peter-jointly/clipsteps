🛒 Ingredients — ✓ from Julie's written recipe (makes 12 pieces)

  • 2 zucchini, grated — you're after about 3 cups of grated veg all up besides the onion (her on-camera rule)
  • 1 large brown onion, finely diced
  • 3 rashers bacon, finely chopped — from your butcher, or leave it out (see Step 3)
  • 1 cup (120 g) tasty cheese, grated
  • 1 cup (150 g) self-raising flour — gluten-free SR flour swaps in cup-for-cup (see Step 7)
  • 1 tsp salt · ½ tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ cup (125 ml) vegetable or olive oil
  • 5 eggs
  • Optional mix-ins from her note: corn kernels (she adds corn in the video), grated carrot, spring onion, shredded beans

🧰 Equipment · ⏱ Timing

  • A lamington tin, about 30 × 20 cm (her written recipe: 18 × 28 cm base) · spray oil + baking paper · a box grater, big bowl and whisk · a cooling rack
  • About 10 minutes hands-on, then 170°C for 35–40 minutes (the video says 25–30 — see Step 9)
  • Makes 12 generous pieces — breakfast, lunchbox or dinner. Freezer win: it defrosts beautifully, so Julie usually makes a double or triple batch
