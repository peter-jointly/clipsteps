🛒 Ingredients (the design brief: use what's in your pantry)

  • Fruit base: rhubarb and apples (pears or tinned apples/peaches work too) + a handful of frozen raspberries if you have them
  • Sugars: caster sugar and raw sugar
  • Topping: plain flour, oats, butter, nuts (pistachios in the demo)
  • Flavour: cinnamon and vanilla
  • To serve: icing sugar, maple syrup, vanilla ice cream

🧰 Equipment · ⏱ Timing

  • A saucepan, a large baking tin and/or small ramekins, and (optionally) a food processor
  • Oven at 180°C; bake 20–30 minutes — ramekins are ready before a big tin
  • About 20 minutes hands-on, under an hour end to end
