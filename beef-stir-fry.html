🛒 Ingredients — ✓ from Jack's written list in the video description (serves 2–3)

  • Jasmine rice: 1 cup (200 g) jasmine rice, washed · 1½ cups (375 ml) cold water · salt to taste · 10 g coriander, roughly chopped (folded through at the end)
  • Steak & seasoning: 400 g flank, skirt, rump, fillet or scotch steak, thinly sliced · salt to taste · cracked black pepper to taste (roughly 1 tsp / 2 g)
  • Stir-fry:
    • 3 tbsp (60 ml) peanut oil, split 2 tbsp (40 ml) + 1 tbsp (20 ml) — vegetable oil if peanuts are an allergy issue (Jack's own swap)
    • 1 large brown onion, sliced · 1 small red capsicum, sliced · 100 g snow peas, stalks and rib removed (the video grabs 120 g — see Step 3)
    • 3 garlic cloves, grated · 10 g ginger, grated
    • 1 tbsp (20 ml) soy sauce · 1 tbsp (20 ml) dark soy sauce (gluten-free: tamari works for both — check the oyster/hoisin label too)
    • 1½ tbsp (30 ml) oyster or hoisin sauce
    • 1¼ tbsp (25 ml) Shaoxing wine (contains alcohol — Jack's note: substitute stock, or leave it out)
    • ¼ cup (60 ml) beef or chicken stock, or water (the video pours ~100 ml — see Step 9)
    • 1½ tbsp (6 g) cornflour · 1 tsp (2 g) cracked black pepper
    • Coriander (optional) · 1 long red chilli, sliced (optional)

🧰 Equipment · ⏱ Timing

  • A wok on your hottest burner (or your biggest heavy-based frypan), plus a small saucepan with a lid for the rice
  • Microplane or fine grater for the garlic-ginger paste · mortar and pestle for the peppercorns — a pepper grinder does the same job (Jack's own alternative)
  • A jug or bowl and a whisk for the sauce
  • Under 30 minutes all-in (the title's promise): rice 12 min + 6 min steam off the heat; the wok work itself is about 8 minutes end to end
  • Serves 2–3 as written (stated in the video) — feeding four: scale everything up and sear the beef in extra batches (Step 11) so the wok stays ripping hot
  • Kitchen win: the sauce is a pantry-shelf formula, and Jack's own recipe note says it's just as good with chicken, seafood or fish — learn it once, repeat it forever
